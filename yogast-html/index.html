JUDO

Our judo program focuses on teaching fundamental techniques, including throws, pins, and joint locks, while emphasizing the principles of discipline, respect, and sportsmanship. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics or an experienced practitioner aiming to refine your skills, our club provides the resources and support you need to achieve your goals.

ATHLETICS

Our athletics program offers a variety of events and disciplines, catering to athletes of all ages and abilities. From sprinting to long-distance running, from high jump to javelin throw, we provide comprehensive training and coaching to help athletes excel in their chosen events

FOOTBALL

We provide top-notch coaching, state-of-the-art facilities, and a supportive community where players can hone their skills, build teamwork, and foster a love for the game.
